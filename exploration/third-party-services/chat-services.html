Chatra

Chatra is a live chat messenger app for your website.

  • Enable Chatra →
  • Activate sidebar button

Visit Dashboard to get your ChatraID.

After Chatra enabled, you can set chat.enable to true and set chat.service to chatra in theme config file.

Tidio

Tidio is a live chat messenger app for your website.

  • Enable Tidio →
  • Activate sidebar button

Visit Dashboard to get your Public Key.

After Tidio enabled, you can set chat.enable to true and set chat.service to tidio in theme config file.

I’m Gloria, a first-year master student at Computer Science Department, Stanford University. Previously I studied at Tsinghua University and obtained my first master’s degree in Computer Science and Information Art and Design, during which I was broadly exposed to collaborative projects with the industry, involving user experience design, UI design and so on. My research projects range from business and serious games, to inclusive design with a focus on visually impaired people, then to the interaction between user and data. Though seemingly a mixed background, I was always trying to move towards the balance between users’ emotions and their effective productivity, as well as how design and technology can be better combined to this endeavor.
